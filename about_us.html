About Us

Shiv Kalyan Kendra, as an NGO was established in 1986 by Vishwa Hindu Parishad (Kokan Pranth) at Hanuman Tekdi, Sion Koliwada, Mumbai.

The main objectives of Shiv Kalyan Kendra was to develop the erstwhile Salamati Hills (Hanuman Tekdi) for social cause which was being used mostly for anti-social activities as this was totally neglected and surrounded by slums that lacked basic facilities during that time.

We observed that this project being mostly among slums, the main challenges for the people was Employment, poor education & health care infrastructure apart from other issues. Hence, Shiv Kalyan Kendra has been running many projects to support the needs of the surrounding locality :

Our Mission

Ambient space and teaching faculty to help poor students study
Provide clean & hygienic accommodations, food & transportation for poor cancer patients
Free Consultation with Oncologist 24/7 Ambulance facility
Setup Charitable Dialysis & Diagnostic centre Mobile Dispensary at surrounding slum areas
Setup Generic Pharmacy

Our Vision

Provide professionally managed Educational & Health Care facilities for poor & underprivileged people at a nominal cost.
